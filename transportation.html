Transportation

Getting to Boston

Trains

Amtrak is a good option if you are coming from New York or north of Boston. South Station, Back Bay Station, and North Station are all within Boston proper.

Planes

If you are flying in for the wedding, Boston Logan Airport (BOS) is the best option. From there, you can take a quick taxi, Uber, or Lyft to the city. Logan is also on the Blue and Silver lines of the T (Boston's subway system), which will take you into downtown Boston.

Automobiles

We trust you to use Google Maps to get to Boston. We would encourage you to drive carefully around the city as there are a lot of one-way streets and GPS signals occasionally get disrupted in the tunnels under downtown Boston.

Getting around Boston

Once you get to Boston, you won't need a car. (Boston is notoriously difficult to drive in!) Uber & Lyft are widely available. The T (Boston's subway system) is also an option. If you take the T to the wedding, the venue is right next to the Longwood stop on the D branch (towards Riverside) of the Green line.
